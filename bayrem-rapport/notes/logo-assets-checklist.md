## üìê Logo Specifications

**Dimensions:** 
- Small icons: 24x24px to 32x32px
- Medium logos: 48x48px to 64x64px  
- Large logos: 96x96px to 128x128px

**Format:**
- Primary: PNG with transparency
- Backup: SVG for scalability
- Background: Transparent or white

**Naming Convention:**
```
assets/logos/[technology]-logo.png
assets/logos/[technology]-logo.svg
```
